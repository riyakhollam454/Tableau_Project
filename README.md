Plush Super Store Data Analysis Dashboard
 Overview
The **Plush Super Store Data Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence report that visualizes key sales and profit metrics across multiple dimensions such as **product category, subcategory, region, city, and customer segments**.  
It helps stakeholders analyze performance trends, identify top revenue sources, and make data-driven decisions to improve profitability and efficiency.

Dashboard Components

1. **Sales by Category**
A bar chart displaying total sales across major categories:
- **Technology** generates the highest sales.
- **Furniture** and **Office Supplies** follow closely behind.

 2. **Subcategory by Sales Percentile**
A pie chart showing the proportion of sales by each product subcategory, helping identify which subcategories contribute most to overall revenue.

3. **Category-Wise Profit by Month**
A line chart that tracks monthly profit trends by subcategory.  
It reveals patterns, seasonality, and performance fluctuations over time.

4. **Sales by Region**
A bar chart that compares total sales by region:
- **West** is the top-performing region.
- **East**, **Central**, and **South** regions follow in order.

 5. **Sales by Region and Segment**
Breaks down sales by both **region** and **customer segment** (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).  
Useful for understanding which customer types drive the most sales in each region.

 6. **Sales by City**
Displays the top cities by total sales:
- **New York City** leads significantly.
- Followed by **Los Angeles**, **Seattle**, and **San Francisco**.

 7. **Sales and Profit by Customer**
A scatter plot representing the relationship between **sales** and **profit** for individual customers.  
Each dot represents a customer, identifying the most valuable and least profitable ones.

 8. **Geographical Map Visualization**
An interactive map highlighting sales distribution across U.S. states.  
This allows users to quickly identify high-performing and low-performing regions.

 Filter Feature
- **Month of Order Date Filter:**  
  Enables users to view performance metrics for a specific month, providing flexibility for time-based analysis.

 Key Insights
- **Technology** products generate the most revenue.  
- **West Region** consistently outperforms others in total sales.  
- **New York City** is the top-selling city across all categories.  
- **Consumer Segment** contributes the most to sales compared to Corporate and Home Office.  
- Some subcategories experience profit fluctuations, indicating areas for margin optimization.

Tools & Technology
- **Visualization Tool:** Tableau  
- **Data Source:** Plush Super Store Sales Dataset (Sample Data)  
- **Visual Elements:** Bar Chart, Line Chart, Pie Chart, Scatter Plot, and Map  

Purpose
This dashboard serves as a comprehensive tool for:
- Monitoring store performance across multiple dimensions.  
- Identifying key revenue drivers and problem areas.  
- Supporting management in strategic planning and forecasting.  
- Enhancing data transparency and decision-making efficiency.
